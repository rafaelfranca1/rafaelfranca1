# 👋 Hi there

I'm Rafael de França, a curious and analytical software developer passionate about backend development and software engineering.  
My journey into technology started with curiosity — I’ve always been fascinated by how things work behind the scenes. That curiosity led me to Computer Science, where I discovered in software development a space to constantly learn, solve problems, and create solutions that positively impact people and businesses.  

Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Computer Science and building my career in backend development. My main goal is to become a reference professional in software engineering, mastering the technologies I work with while staying open to new knowledge.  

Outside of programming, I enjoy games and music, which keep me creative and inspired.  

# 🚀 Expertise

My expertise lies primarily in backend development.  
I’ve worked on REST APIs, personal projects, and business systems, always focusing on clean architecture and maintainable code.  

I have hands-on experience with PostgreSQL, using tools like pgAdmin and DBeaver, and I frequently work with ORM frameworks such as Hibernate and Prisma to bridge the gap between databases and application logic.  

Even though I’m still early in my career, I’ve developed a strong foundation in Java, Spring Boot and NestJS. While my main focus is to deepen my expertise in the technologies I already master, I remain open to learning and expanding into new areas.
